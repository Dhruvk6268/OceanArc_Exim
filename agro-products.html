Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our agriculture products and export process

What quality standards do your agriculture products meet?

All our agriculture products meet international quality standards including FSSAI, ISO, and other relevant certifications. We conduct regular quality checks and laboratory testing to ensure purity, safety, and compliance with destination country regulations.

What is the minimum order quantity for agriculture products?

Minimum order quantities vary by product. We offer flexibility to accommodate both small and large-scale orders. Contact our team with your specific requirements, and we'll provide customized solutions that match your business needs.

How do you ensure product freshness during international shipping?

We use specialized packaging materials and techniques designed for agricultural products. Our packaging solutions include moisture barriers, temperature control where necessary, and proper ventilation to maintain product freshness throughout the shipping process.

Do you offer organic agriculture products?

Yes, we offer a range of certified organic agriculture products. Our organic portfolio includes rice, pulses, and other staples that are grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. All our organic products carry appropriate certifications.

What is your typical delivery timeline for international orders?

Delivery timelines depend on the destination, product type, and order quantity. Typically, orders are processed within 3-5 business days, with shipping times ranging from 15-30 days depending on the destination. We provide detailed timelines during the order confirmation process.
